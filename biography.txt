Antoine Madec is Director of Verification at Axelera AI, where he leads ASIC verification for next-generation AI accelerator chips.
With over 15 years of experience spanning both sides of the Atlantic, he has held roles as technical lead, manager, and applications engineer at companies including Microsoft Xbox, Tesla, Ampere, and Bosch.

Antoine specializes in system-level verification and the development of emulation platforms, with a practical philosophy centered on simplicity and efficiency over complexity.
At Axelera AI, he drives verification strategy across multiple tape-outs, from architecture through silicon bring-up.
